SUMMIT

Communication Summit is a dominant dais for peer-to-peer learning and knowledge sharing, bringing together leading communicators and marketing professionals from companies, agencies , associations, NGOs and institutions to network and discuss ideas on the most cutting-edge tools and strategies in the field. We invite marketers to join us and to celebrate the art of communication and marketing.

Speakers

Fredrik Härén

Author and Keynote Speaker on
Business Creativity, Innovation, Change and Global Business

Fredrik Härén

He has delivered 2,000 presentations in over 60 countries on 6 continents. (Just last year he spoke in 22 different countries.) He was voted Speaker of tyhe Year in Sweden as well as selected as one of the "The Best Swedish Speakers Ever". He is a CSP (Certified Speaking Professional) (one of 700 globally) as well as GSP Global (one of only 30 globally). He is the author of 9 books, including "The Idea Book" which was included in "The 100 Best Business Books of All Time".

Kaushik Roy

President- – Brand Strategy & Marketing Communication
Reliance Industries Limited (RIL)

Kaushik Roy

Kaushik Roy has spent over three decades in the sphere of media and communication. In recent times, he has been responsible for the branding, marketing and team formation of Reliance Mumbai Indians for the Indian Premier League (IPL) Season 1, and the launch of the Indian Super League (ISL). As a sponsor from the Chairman’s office, he oversees the marketing and branding efforts for Jio, an integrated digital content and services offering from RIL.
In his marketing avatar, he has worked as Head of Brand Strategy, South Asia with Philips and later with Reliance Infocomm as Head – Branding and Marketing Communication.

Nirvik Singh

Chairman & CEO
Grey Group Asia Pacific

Nirvik Singh

Nirvik is a 27-year marketing and communication industry veteran and has served as Chairman & CEO of Grey Group Asia Pacific since 2009. He began his career with Lipton India, a Unilever company. He joined Grey Group as head of Grey Kolkata at the age of 26. In short order, the agency grew to become the city’s second largest, producing some of India’s most path-breaking campaigns, and winning Agency of the Year four consecutive times.
As Chairman & CEO of Grey Group Asia Pacific, he oversees 20 geographies that include Japan, China and Australia, with a staff of over 2000 people.

Bharat Avalani

CEO
Connecting the Dots Consultancy

BHARAT AVALANI

Bharat Avalani is a Unilever Veteran who comes with rich experience and expertise in Brand Management, Media Strategy, Brand Activation, Market Development and Integrated Brand Communications. He was the former Regional IBC Director for Unilever Homecare category across Asia, Africa, Middle East & Turkey. He has served as President of the International Advertising Association (IAA) Malaysian Chapter, as VP of the Malaysian Advertisers Association (MAA), and as a Board Member of the Audit Bureau of Circulations (ABC) Malaysia. Bharat is currently on the Executive committee of the Asian Federation of Advertising Associations (AFAA) & IAA Malaysia.

Fazal Ashfaq

Country Lead for Bangladesh and Sri-lanka
Google

Fazal Ashfaq

Fazal Ashfaq is an Industry Analyst for South Asia Emerging Markets at Google. He provides research & analytical support to Google's Sales & Business development team for Bangladesh, Pakistan & Sri Lanka. He is also the project lead for the recently launched Google Bus Bangladesh initiative - a year long effort to educate half a million students across Bangladesh on the power and utility of the Internet.
